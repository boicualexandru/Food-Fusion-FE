How to create an account?

Step 1: Press 'Login' button on the main page.

Step 2: After you are redirected on Login page, press on 'Sign up' button.

Step 3. Add an valid email addres on first field. On the second one add the last name and the first name. The password should be added on the thitd field.

Step 4: Press 'Sign up' button.

Step 5: The account is successfully created.

How to make a reservation?

Step 1: Login using your account.

Step 2: Chooose a restaurant for reservation.

Step 3. Choose the number of persons.

Step 4: Select the date for rezervation using MM/DD/YYYY format.

Step 5: Add the hour for starting and ending the reservation.

Step 6: If you want, you can choose a table pressing 'Choose Table' button.

Step 7: To finish the reservation, press 'Book' button.
